What is Migraine?

General characteristics

A migraine is a severe neurological disorder similar to a severe headache. It that causes acute throbbing pain or a pulsing sensation, usually on one side of the head, extending to the eyes and forehead. Migraine attacks can last for 2-72 hours, and often interferes with migraine sufferer's daily activities.

Symptoms often include:

  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • extreme sensitvity to
    • light
    • sound
  • auras (eg. blinking light)
  • numbness in the
    • limbs
    • face
    • tongue
    • face
